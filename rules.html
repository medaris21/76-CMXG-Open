Tournament Rules

Team Format

Each team in the CMXG Open consists of 4 players competing together throughout the tournament.

Teams are formed based on registration. All team members must be registered before the tournament begins.

Tee Off

At the beginning of each hole, all four team members hit a tee shot.

The order of play is determined by team strategy, but all players must complete their tee shots before proceeding to the next stage of play.

Best Ball Selection

After all tee shots, the team will collectively choose the best shot from their four attempts.

Once selected:

  • All players play their next shot from this chosen location
  • Team members may discuss and agree on the best shot location
  • Selection must be made within 3 minutes to maintain pace of play

Ball Placement

After selecting the best shot, players must place their ball within specific parameters:

  • Within one club length of the selected shot position
  • Must remain in the same type of lie (fairway, rough, bunker, etc.)
  • On the green, place the ball within one putter head length

Players may not improve their lie beyond what the placement rules allow. If the selected shot is in a hazard, all subsequent shots must be played from within that hazard.

Scoring

The team continues selecting the best shot and playing from that spot until the ball is holed.

Key scoring rules:

  • Only one score per team per hole is recorded
  • The score is the total number of strokes taken from tee to hole
  • Scores must be recorded immediately after completing each hole
  • Both players and a tournament official must sign each scorecard

Tee Shot Requirement

To ensure all team members contribute:

  • Each player must use at least 2 tee shots during the round
  • Team captains must designate which tee shots will count for each player before the round
  • Failure to meet this requirement results in a 2-stroke penalty per missing tee shot

This rule ensures all team members participate actively throughout the tournament.

Mulligans

Mulligans are available for purchase at select holes to improve your golfing ability:

  • Each mulligan allows a player to re-take one shot without penalty
  • Mulligans can be purchased at designated holes throughout the course
  • Cost: different per mulligan (proceeds go to charity)

Mulligans must be declared before the next shot is taken and cannot be used on putts.
